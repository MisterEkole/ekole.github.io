About

Let me introduce myself.

I am Mitterrand Ekole, a final year Mechatronics Engineering Student from the College of Technology of the University of Buea Cameroon. Passionate about Artificial Intelligence I am on a quest to bridge the gap between engineering and medicine. Seeking via advanced research how electromechanical systems, and mechatronics systems can be powered with AI to solve compelling societal problems.

My sole field of research has been based on bio-mechatronics where i seek to find state of the art means through which medicine meets AI and mechatronics, specifically in the design of prosthetics ,bionic devices and disease prediction systems where i have been working on clear cut machine learning technologies to detect various health diseases and anomalies.

In addition to the above mentioned, I am also a UI/UX designer, engineering product designer, and tech writer. Reading, cooking, listening to music, watching documentaries(tech and politics oriented) are my hobbies. Always willing to learn and open for more opportunities.
